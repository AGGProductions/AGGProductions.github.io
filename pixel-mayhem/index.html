Description

Pixel Mayhem is a fast-paced, top-down survival game where you play as a small pixel that is trying to survive in a room filled with randomly moving obstacles. Your objective is to avoid the obstacles and reach the other side of the room to progress to the next level. The obstacles may be stationary or moving, and they may vary in size and shape. As you progress through the levels, the obstacles become more complex and challenging.

To help you survive, you can collect power-ups throughout the game. These power-ups include a shield that protects you from obstacles, a speed boost that allows you to move faster, and a slow-down power-up that slows down the obstacles.

Pixel Mayhem is a game that tests your reflexes and strategic thinking. It's easy to learn but challenging to master, and it's perfect for anyone who loves fast-paced, action-packed games. With its simple yet addictive game play and colorful pixel art graphics, Pixel Mayhem is a game that you won't be able to put down!
