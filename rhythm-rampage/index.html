Description

Prepare yourself for a rhythm adventure in Rhythm Rampage

Notes rain down the screen, it's up to you to time yourself to hit each note with precision and flair.

Discover a selection of tracks from various genres, each chosen to challenge your timing and reflexes. master the art of rhythm to achieve the highest score on every track!

Play Rhythm Rampage on your mobile device or PC, ensuring you can always have your rhythm fix, whether you're on the go or at home.

Compete for the highest score and strive to beat your personal best on each track. Aim for perfection as you win your way to victory.

While there isn't a level editor built-in, you have the freedom to create custom levels using your favorite MIDI program. Craft intricate challenges and share them with the community to test their rhythm skills.

Can you rise to the rhythmical challenge and make your mark in the world of Rhythm Rampage?
